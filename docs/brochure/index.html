How it works
Tokenise. Stream. Distribute.
01 · Tokenise

Upload a video and mint a BSV-21 content token. Sell licences to creators, backers, and seeders.

02 · Stream

Viewers open a BSV payment channel and stream pieces directly from peers. Every piece verified, every satoshi signed on-chain.

03 · Distribute

Payments fan out to every token holder in real time. 100% of revenue, zero platform cut, no intermediaries.
